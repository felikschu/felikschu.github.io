About / 关于本站

这个站点是我学习大模型可解释性（Mechanistic Interpretability）的公开笔记，也是一份写给所有对 AI 内部机制感到好奇之人的邀请函。

在大模型的发展浪潮中，Anthropic 对可解释性投入了极大的热情与无可比拟的重视。从 Transformer Circuits 的数学框架，到 Toy Models of Superposition 对特征叠加的揭示，再到后来的 Circuit Tracing 与 Model Diffing，他们正执着地试图把庞大的“黑盒”一点点拆解成可以理解的“白盒”。然而在国内，这个领域似乎并没有得到同等的重视，相关的系统性研究和讨论依然显得有些冷清。

我希望能尽自己的微薄之力，做一点真诚的记录与开源，哪怕只是为国内的讨论增加一点点微弱的声音。

但坦白说，我之所以被这门学科深深吸引，初衷其实非常个人。在 AI 狂飙突进的进程面前，人类个体时常会感到一种强烈的渺小感——我们似乎正在被时代飞速地抛在身后。但我始终觉得，人类最宝贵的品质，就是面对未知的好奇心以及对事物本质的纯粹渴求，这始终是我们向上攀登的阶梯。

大模型可解释性，恰好就是那个能极大地激起我这种好奇心的事物。那些庞大、幽暗、似乎只由统计规律支配的参数矩阵深处，竟然隐藏着如此精密的几何结构与逻辑回路。

我确乎只是一个刚入门的无名小卒。建立这个站点，并不敢奢求能给人类的 AI 发展做出什么宏大的贡献。你可以把这里当作是一个普通人，在被 AI 远远抛在后面的时代间隙里，忙里偷闲地停下脚步，去凝视和拆解一下 AI 运作的本质。

不为别的，一切单纯由好奇心驱动。如果你也同样着迷于这些巨型矩阵深处的齿轮与回路，欢迎你在这里驻足。

🇬🇧 English Version

This site is a public notebook of my journey into Mechanistic Interpretability, and an invitation to anyone who shares a deep curiosity about the inner workings of AI.

Amidst the tidal wave of Large Language Models, Anthropic has placed an immense and unparalleled emphasis on interpretability. From the mathematical framework of Transformer Circuits to the revelation of feature superposition in Toy Models, and later to Circuit Tracing and Model Diffing, they are relentlessly striving to dismantle the massive "black box" into an understandable "white box." However, here in China, this field seems to receive significantly less attention, and systematic research and discussion remain somewhat sparse.

I hope to offer my modest efforts to keep a sincere, open-source record, if only to add a faint voice to the domestic conversation.

Yet, frankly speaking, my fascination with this discipline stems from a profoundly personal place. In the face of AI's explosive and relentless progress, it is easy to feel a profound sense of human insignificance—we seem to be rapidly left behind by the era. But I have always believed that humanity's most precious quality is our curiosity towards the unknown and our pure thirst for the essence of things. This has always been the ladder of human progress.

Mechanistic Interpretability is exactly the subject that profoundly ignites this curiosity in me. It is fascinating to discover that deep within those massive, obscure parameter matrices—seemingly governed only by statistical laws—hide such precise geometric structures and logical circuits.

I am, essentially, just a nobody who recently entered this field. By building this site, I harbor no grand illusions of making a monumental contribution to human AI development. You can simply think of this space as the work of an ordinary person who, in the cracks of an era where AI is leaving us far behind, steals a quiet moment of leisure to pause, gaze into, and unravel the true nature of how AI operates.

For no other reason, it is driven purely by curiosity. If you are also mesmerized by the gears and circuits hidden deep within these giant matrices, welcome. Stay a while.
